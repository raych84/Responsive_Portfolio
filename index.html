About Me

My name is, Rachel Lauren Williams. "It was never easy for me. I was born a poor black child. I remember the days sitting on the porch with my family, singing and dancing, down in Mississippi."
-Navin R. Johnson
The Jerk

Seriously, I was born on Guam. I'm 36 years young. I've lived in Florida since 1994. I graduated from Seminole High School in 2002. I previously worked in customer service as a Employee Benefits Administrator. I left that job after nine years and started as a software QA analyst. The company that I worked for wanted all of their QA Analysts to learn automation, and I was bit by the coding bug. I decided to take this course to that I could learn how to write automated tests and eventually look into becoming an SDET.
