About Me

The Jerk
My name is Rachel Williams. I was born on Guam. I'm 36 years young. I've lived in Florida since 1994. I graduated from Seminole High School in 2002. I attended Seminole Community College, now known as, Seminole State College, majoring in elementary education. After having some exposure to kindergarten aged children, I came to realize, that was not the best career choice for me.
I previously worked in customer service as an Employee Benefits Administrator. I left that job after nine years and started as a software QA analyst. The company that I worked for wanted all of their QA Analysts to learn automation, and I was bit by the coding bug. I decided to take this course so that I could learn how to write automated tests and eventually look into becoming an SDET.

It took me forever, but I'm finally done!
